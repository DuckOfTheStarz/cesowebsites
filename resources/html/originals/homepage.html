Researching precariousness across the paid/unpaid work continuum. ERC Advanced Grant

This project is funded by the European Research Council (ERC) under the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme (grant agreement n° 833577).  ResPecTMe will generate a new theoretical model of, and a measurement approach and monitoring tools for, precariousness at the paid and unpaid work continuum.​​​​​​​​
