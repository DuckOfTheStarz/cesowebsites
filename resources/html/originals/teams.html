The RespectMe Team

Headed by prof. Valeria Pulignano, our team consists of researchers and one administrative staff member. Together with our project partners, we will perform and disseminate research on the paid and unpaid work continuum.

Publications

Our researchers are part of the Centre for Sociological Research at KU Leuven and closely cooperate with the research team New Perspectives on Employment & Labour.  In addition, we receive administrative support from the secretariat of the centre.
